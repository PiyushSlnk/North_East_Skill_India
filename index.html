Frequently Asked Questions

It is crucial to check geographic flight zones to ensure compliance with regulations and avoid entering restricted areas. This helps prevent legal issues and ensures safety during UAV operations.

  • 25 km around international borders
  • 10 km around airports
  • 1 km around government buildings
  • 1 km around minister houses

Flying UAVs in these areas is restricted and considered a punishable offense.

Temporary no-fly zones will be declared for public meetings and events. UAV operators are advised to follow these restrictions to ensure compliance and avoid penalties.

Special permission will be provided to registered government and private agencies for defined purposes. These permissions are issued based on the specific needs and conditions of the operation.

Using UAVs for malicious activities, including illegal video or photographs, is a punishable offense. Such actions are subject to legal penalties and enforcement actions.

From 01.01.2025 onwards, the selling, purchasing, or operating of foreign UAV brands (like DJI) without a Unique Identification Number (UIN) is completely banned in the state.

2. Trade License Application Categories (Information Tab)

Manufacturer

Covered Area: Minimum 2,000 sq.ft. with power supply, drinking water, and sanitary facility.

Area Ownership: Must be owned or leased (agreement copy required).

Annual Turnover: Over 50 lakhs.

Owner Residency: Must be a resident of Sikkim.

Employee Requirement: 20 employees, with at least 50% being residents of Sikkim.

UAV Stock: At least 10 units, combining micro and/or small category UAVs, in ready stock.

Service Provider

Covered Area: Minimum 1,000 sq.ft. with power supply, drinking water, and sanitary facility.

Area Ownership: Must be owned or leased (agreement copy required).

Annual Turnover: Over 20 lakhs.

Owner Residency: Must be a resident of Sikkim.

Employee Requirement: 10 employees, with at least 50% being residents of Sikkim.

UAV Stock: At least 5 units, combining micro and/or small category UAVs, in ready stock to conduct services.

Drone Training School Registration (As per DGCA)

Annual Turnover: Over 20 lakhs.

Open Sky Playgrounds: 2 playgrounds, each 60*60 meters.

Classrooms: 4 classrooms, each 300 sq.ft., with electricity supply.

Facilities: Washroom, electricity, and drinking water.

Equipment: 10 computers, 1 TV.

Staff: 2 licensed instructors and 1 account manager.

Area Ownership: Must be owned or leased (agreement copy required).

Owner Residency: Must be a resident of Sikkim.

UAV Store / Shop Owner / Distributor & Repairing Center

Commercial Space: 500 sq.ft. electronic store.

Store Authorization: OEM authorized stores.

Area Ownership: Must be owned or leased (agreement copy required).

Annual Turnover: Over 15 lakhs.

Owner Residency: Must be a resident of Sikkim.

Employee Requirement: 2 employees, with 100% being residents of Sikkim.
